The Firm

Built on
objective truth.

The name InVerum derives from the Latin verum, meaning objective truth. Most operational problems have a right answer. The job is to find it without agenda, design for it without compromise, and build it without waste. That is the only operating principle here.

The firm was built by a practitioner, not a consultant. Doug Bonebrake spent nearly a decade as a product owner and product manager at Cerner and Oracle Health, two of the largest enterprise health IT platforms in the world. He has seen what scale looks like from the inside, and what happens when organizations build for complexity instead of clarity.

Lean operational methodology is not a framework invoked at kickoff; it is embedded in every assessment InVerum conducts.

He currently operates multiple concurrent technology ventures including a clinical trial sample management platform, logistics technology engagements, and AI-powered diagnostic tooling for a London-based firm. The advice is current, tested, and grounded in conditions that are live right now.
